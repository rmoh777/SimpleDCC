Brand Guidelines Summary

  • Always maintain the 0.5rem gap between icon and text
  • Always use the emerald gradient for the icon background
  • Always highlight "CC" in emerald green (#10b981)
  • Always use system fonts for consistency
  • Never separate the icon from the text
  • Never change the radar emoji or use alternative icons
  • Never use colors outside the approved palette
  • Never distort proportions or stretch the logo
